One of the first questions people ask when planning an event is: "What's this going to cost me?" It's a fair question — and honestly, the vague non-answers you'll find on most bartending sites are frustrating. Here's a transparent, honest breakdown of how mobile bartending is priced in Cincinnati, so you can plan with confidence before you even reach out for a quote.

What Affects the Price?

No two events are exactly alike, and your quote will reflect the specific details of yours. Here are the main factors that shape what you'll pay:

  • Guest count — More guests means faster service demands. For larger parties, an additional bartender may be recommended to keep lines short and energy high.
  • Event duration — The number of hours we're on-site directly affects the overall service cost.
  • Package selected — Our Golden Standard and Signature packages are priced differently based on what's included.
  • Add-ons — Extras like a signature cocktail, coffee bar, decorative arch, or glassware upgrade each add value (and a corresponding cost) to your event.
  • Travel distance — We're based in downtown Cincinnati (45202). Events within 30 miles round-trip are included; beyond that, travel fees apply.
  • Day of week and season — Peak wedding season (May–October) and weekend events are in highest demand and may affect availability and pricing.

What You're NOT Paying For (The Dry-Hire Advantage)

Here's where 513Sips can save you serious money compared to a traditional full-service bar setup: we're a dry-hire service. That means we provide all the skilled bartending, equipment, and expertise — but you supply the alcohol.

With 513Sips, you avoid:

  • Alcohol markups — You buy at retail price from your preferred store. No middle-man margin.
  • Hidden bar fees — The quote you get is the quote you pay. No surprise line items.
  • Per-drink pricing — Flat-rate service, not a running tab charged to you by the bottle.

Choosing a dry-hire model can save you 30–50% compared to a traditional full-service bar when you factor in retail alcohol pricing vs. vendor-marked-up liquor costs.

Don't want to deal with the shopping? We offer an Alcohol Procurement add-on where we handle purchasing at cost plus a flat service fee — giving you the savings of retail pricing without the legwork.
