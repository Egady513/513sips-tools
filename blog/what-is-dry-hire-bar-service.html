What Is a Dry-Hire Bar Service and Why Is It Perfect for You?

Everything Cincinnati hosts need to know before booking a bartender — and why keeping control of your own bar is almost always the smarter move.

April 17, 2026 By Eddie Gady · 513Sips 6 min read

If you've started looking into bar service for a Cincinnati event — whether it's a graduation party, a corporate happy hour, or a wedding reception — you've probably come across the term "dry-hire." It gets thrown around a lot, but not everyone explains what it actually means or why it might be the smartest decision you make for your event.

So let's clear it up. Here's everything you need to know about dry-hire bar service, how it stacks up against a traditional full-service bar, and why it's become the go-to choice for hosts who want professional bartending without paying a premium for someone else's liquor inventory.

What Is a Dry-Hire Bar Service?

Dry-hire means we provide the bar, the bartenders, and the experience. Alcohol is purchased at cost for the event — either by you, or by 513Sips as a simple add-on. No up-charges, no profiting off alcohol, no alcohol sales.

Ohio's liquor licensing system is built around permanent establishments (restaurants, bars, venues), and there's no "event license" a mobile bartender can grab to start selling bottles at your backyard party. So instead of finding a workaround, we built our whole business around it: we serve, you supply. And that setup quietly saves you a lot of money.

The biggest markup in the bar industry — the one that turns a $22 bottle into a $70+ open bar charge — doesn't exist with 513Sips. You buy at Costco, Party Source, or Jungle Jim's prices. We bring everything else: the full bar setup, ice, mixers, certified bartenders, and the experience to make it all feel effortless.

If you'd rather not handle the shopping yourself, we offer it as an add-on — we'll work with you to curate exactly what your event needs and handle the run. And if there's anything left over, it's yours to take home or we'll take care of returning it for you — either way, you don't have to think about it.

We serve clients across Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky — and our dry-hire model keeps every event simple and legally clean regardless of which side of the river you're on. You're not doing this alone. We've worked enough events to know exactly what yours needs, and we'll walk you through every step.

Why Dry-Hire Is Perfect for Cincinnati Events

There are plenty of reasons Cincinnati hosts are choosing dry-hire over traditional full-service bars. Here are the five that come up most often:

  • 1
    Significant Cost Savings When you buy alcohol at retail — from Jungle Jim's, Total Wine, or your favorite local shop — you pay the actual price for each bottle. Traditional full-service bars markup that same alcohol by 30–50% or more to cover their overhead and profit margin. With dry-hire, you eliminate that markup entirely. For a 100-person event, that can translate to hundreds of dollars staying in your pocket.
  • 2
    Complete Flexibility and Control Want to stock exclusively Cincinnati-brewed craft beers? A specific bottle of bourbon that's meaningful to your family? A natural wine selection that fits your values? With dry-hire, you decide exactly what's served at your event. There's no being locked into a vendor's limited menu or house selections. You curate, we serve.
  • 3
    Ohio Liquor Law Compliance Selling alcohol in Ohio requires a liquor license — period. Dry-hire is the legally compliant path for private events because the bartending service isn't selling or supplying the alcohol, just serving it on the host's behalf. At 513Sips, every event is set up in full compliance with Ohio law so you never have to worry about that piece of it.
  • 4
    A Truly Personalized Experience Dry-hire opens the door to genuine personalization. Planning a wedding? We can build a signature cocktail menu around your story and the spirits you love. Corporate happy hour? Stock local craft brews that reflect Cincinnati pride. A milestone birthday? Feature the guest of honor's favorite drinks. Full-service bars can't easily do this — they're working from a fixed inventory.
  • 5
    Professional Service with a Personal Touch Our bartenders aren't just pouring drinks — they're crafting the experience at your event. Because 513Sips is a boutique, dry-hire operation, your bartenders are fully focused on your guests, not managing a shared inventory across multiple clients. You get genuine engagement, attentive service, and the kind of personal touch that guests remember long after the party ends.
