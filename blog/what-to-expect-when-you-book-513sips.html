What to Expect When You Book 513Sips — From First Inquiry to Last Call

April 17, 2026 By Eddie Gady · 513Sips 5 min read

Booking a mobile bartender for the first time can feel like a mystery. You have a vision for your event, a date circled on the calendar, and a hundred things to coordinate — so the last thing you need is to wonder what happens after you hit send. Here's a plain-language, step-by-step look at exactly what working with 513Sips looks like, from the moment you reach out to the moment your guests take their last sip.

1
~5 minutes on your end

You Reach Out

Fill out the contact form, send a text, or shoot an email — whichever feels easiest. This isn't a formal intake process. It's the start of a conversation. We want to get to know you, understand what you're celebrating, and hear what you're envisioning before we say anything back. No pressure, no commitment, no sales pitch. Just two people figuring out if we're a good fit for your event.

2
Within 24 hours

You Receive a Custom Quote

Eddie personally reviews every single inquiry — no automated responses, no generic pricing sheets. Within 24 hours you'll receive a detailed quote that includes package options and transparent add-on pricing. What you see is what you pay. There are no hidden fees, no surprise line items, and no "we'll figure that out later" language. If anything needs clarification, just ask.

3
50% deposit holds your date

Book With a Deposit

Once you're ready to move forward, a 50% deposit officially locks in your date on our calendar. At that point we'll send a service agreement that spells out every detail of your event. The remaining balance is due 14 days before your event. That's it — clean, predictable, and stress-free.

4
Before your event

This Is Where the Magic Happens

This consultation isn't about logistics — it's about making your event feel uniquely yours. We'll build a custom drink menu together, name your signature cocktails, and talk through every detail that makes your celebration different from anyone else's. Past clients have had us design custom bar décor that matched their party theme, create branded drink stirrers, put together koozie sets, and source engraved glassware for the bride and groom.

We'll also go over alcohol quantities — built on 8+ years of bar operations experience — so you buy exactly what you need and nothing goes to waste. Any leftovers can go home with you or we'll handle the return. And we think proactively: for a recent wedding, we arrived early to set up water stations during the ceremony so the wedding party didn't have to think about it. That's the kind of detail that doesn't show up on any invoice — we just do it.

5
90 minutes before service

We Arrive & Set Up

We show up 90 minutes before bar service begins — and we bring everything. The full bar setup, ice, mixers, tools, glassware, and décor elements are all ours to transport, arrange, and handle. You don't need to source a thing. While we're setting up, you focus on getting ready and enjoying the moments before your event starts.

6
Showtime

Service Begins

When the doors open and guests arrive, our bartenders take over completely. Cocktails are crafted with care, guests are welcomed warmly, and the bar becomes the heartbeat of your event. Whether you've curated a signature drink menu or you're keeping it classic, we bring the same level of professionalism and energy to every pour — from the first one to the last.

7
30–45 minutes

Breakdown & Cleanup

After service wraps, we handle every bit of breakdown and cleanup ourselves. Within 30 to 45 minutes we're packed up and out the door, leaving the venue exactly as we found it. All equipment is ours to take — the only thing left behind is the remaining alcohol, which belongs to you. A good time and a clean space. That's the 513Sips standard.

  • Eddie's personal attention on every single event — you're never handed off to someone who doesn't know your details.

  • Fully insured and certified so your venue, your guests, and your peace of mind are protected.
