Behind the Bar

What Does a Mobile Bartender
Actually Do?

April 17, 2026  ·  By Eddie Gady · 513Sips

Most people picture a bartender shaking cocktails behind a fancy setup. But a professional mobile bartender — especially one operating dry-hire — is far more than that. There's planning, logistics, service, safety, and cleanup involved, and when it's done right, it's completely invisible to your guests. Here's a full look at what you actually get when you book 513Sips.

Before the Event

A good mobile bartending experience starts weeks before the first guest arrives. At 513Sips, the pre-event work is where we set your bar up for success.

Planning Phase

We handle the details before day-of

  • Event consultation — we learn your event type, expected guest count, vibe, and any drink preferences or restrictions
  • Menu building — help crafting a drink lineup that fits your event, whether that's signature cocktails, classic crowd-pleasers, or mocktail options
  • Alcohol quantity guidance — we give you a per-head estimate so you buy the right amount and don't run short (or blow your budget overstocking)
  • Venue coordination — we work with your venue to understand setup space, power/water access, and any logistics that matter

Day Of — Arrival & Setup

We arrive approximately 90 minutes before service begins. That's not an accident — it takes real time to set up a professional bar that looks great and runs smoothly from the first drink order.

Setup — ~90 min before guests arrive

We bring everything. You worry about nothing.

  • Bar cart or portable bar setup assembled and dressed to complement your event aesthetic
  • Ice, mixers, garnishes, and bar tools stocked and organized
  • Glassware set up and chilled (when included in your package)
  • Everything fully staged and ready before the first guest walks through the door

During the Event

This is where most people's mental picture kicks in — but there's a lot more happening than just pouring drinks.

Service — start to last call

We run the bar so you can enjoy the party

  • Craft cocktails, beer and wine pours, and custom drink mixes executed with skill and consistency
  • Bar line management — we keep things moving so guests aren't standing around waiting
  • Genuine guest engagement — we remember preferences, make conversation, and help people feel welcome at the bar
  • Responsible service monitoring — we watch for intoxication and comply with Ohio law, including never serving minors under any circumstances
  • Continuous bar maintenance — restocking, cleaning, wiping down, and keeping the setup looking sharp all night

After the Event

The event winds down — and while your guests are saying their goodbyes, we're quietly taking care of everything behind the bar.

Breakdown — ~30 to 45 minutes

We leave your venue cleaner than we found it

  • Full bar breakdown and cleanup — typically 30 to 45 minutes
  • All equipment loaded and taken with us — no mess, no boxes left behind
  • Any remaining alcohol is yours to keep — that's the dry-hire model
