## Emergency Competitive Response

**If Competitor Lowers Prices:**
- Don't match - emphasize value over price
- Highlight full-service advantage
- Offer add-ons instead of discounts

**If New Competitor Enters:**
- Research their positioning within 48 hours
- Monitor their pricing for 30 days
- Differentiate through service, not price

**If Competitor Adds Full Service:**
- Emphasize local ownership
- Highlight customization/flexibility
- Leverage first-mover advantage
