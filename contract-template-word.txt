3. SERVICES PROVIDED
513 Sips provides mobile bar services as outlined in the selected package. This includes professional bartending, setup, takedown, and agreed-upon mixers or décor.

This is a dry-hire bartending service—513 Sips does not purchase, sell, transport, or provide alcohol. The Client or venue must purchase and manage alcoholic beverages in compliance with Ohio law.

4. PAYMENT TERMS
• Deposit: 50% non-refundable deposit due at signing.
• Remaining Balance: Due 14 days before the event.
• Late Payments: Subject to a 15% late fee.
• Payment Methods: Electronic only (Stripe, Venmo Business, Zelle, etc.). No cash or checks.
• Non-Payment: If the remaining balance is not paid by 7 days prior, 513 Sips may cancel with no refund of deposit.

5. SETUP & TAKEDOWN EXPECTATIONS
• Arrival is ~60 minutes before service start for bar assembly and preparation.
• Takedown occurs within 30–45 minutes after service ends.
• Client must provide safe, unobstructed access, adequate lighting, and a clear load-in path.
• Delays caused by venue or Client (locked doors, setup delays, etc.) incur a $50/hour waiting fee (15-minute increments).
• Early arrival beyond 60 minutes may incur a $50/hour labor fee.
• Bartending hours begin when guest service starts.

6. OVERTIME & EXTENSIONS
Events extending past contracted hours are billed at 1.5x standard rate ($112.50/hr per bartender), in full-hour increments.
Overtime must be approved by Client onsite and paid before takedown or invoiced post-event.

7. DAMAGE / MISSING EQUIPMENT
Client is responsible for any loss, theft, or damage to 513 Sips property during the event.
Replacement Values:
• Glassware: $6 per item
• Décor: $25–$150 per item
• Bar Tools: $25 each
• Tent: $400
• Bar Cart: $1,800

Damage will be documented with photos and invoiced within five (5) business days. Payment is due within seven (7) days.

8. TRAVEL & MILEAGE
• Includes 30 miles round-trip from downtown Cincinnati (45202).
• Additional mileage:
  - 31–60 miles round-trip: +$40
  - 61–100 miles round-trip: +$75
  - 100+ miles round-trip: custom quote

9. CANCELLATION POLICY
• 30+ days before event: 75% refund (less deposit)
• 14–29 days: 50% refund (less deposit)
• <14 days: No refund
• All cancellations must be submitted in writing.

If 513 Sips must cancel due to emergency, all payments (including deposit) will be refunded.

If weather creates unsafe conditions and no covered area exists, 513 Sips may pause or cancel service without refund.
