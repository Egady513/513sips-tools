3. SERVICES PROVIDED
513Sips provides mobile bar services as outlined in the selected package. This includes professional bartending, setup, takedown, and agreed-upon mixers or décor.

Dry-Hire Service: 513Sips does not make any profit from alcohol sales. The Client maintains full control over alcohol selection, quantity, and budget. 513Sips is not responsible for purchasing too much or too little alcohol, and all alcohol remains Client's property before, during, and after the event.

By default, Client is responsible for purchasing and transporting all alcoholic beverages in compliance with Ohio law. However, Client may purchase the Alcohol Procurement Add-On, in which case 513Sips will shop for alcohol on Client's behalf using Client's provided budget. Alcohol will be purchased at cost with receipts provided; 513Sips charges only a flat service fee for this convenience.

All service packages include: insured and certified bartenders, complete bar setup and breakdown, ice, mixers, bar tools, and equipment. Glassware may be included depending on package selected.

This dry-hire structure ensures full legal compliance with Ohio liquor laws while providing the convenience of professional bartending service.

4. PAYMENT TERMS
• Deposit: 50% non-refundable deposit due at signing.
• Remaining Balance: Due 14 days before the event.
• Late Payments: Subject to a 15% late fee.
• Payment Methods: Electronic only (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle). No cash or checks.
• Non-Payment: If the remaining balance is not paid by 7 days prior, 513Sips may cancel with no refund of deposit.

5. SETUP & TAKEDOWN EXPECTATIONS
• Arrival is approximately 90 minutes before service start for bar assembly and preparation.
• Takedown occurs within 30–45 minutes after service ends.
• Client must provide safe, unobstructed access, adequate lighting, and a clear load-in path.
• Delays caused by venue or Client (locked doors, setup delays, etc.) incur a $50/hour waiting fee (15-minute increments).
• Bartending hours begin when guest service starts.

6. OVERTIME, EXTENSIONS & EARLY ARRIVAL
Events extending past contracted hours are billed at 1.5x standard rate ($112.50/hr per bartender), in full-hour increments. Overtime must be approved by Client onsite and paid before takedown or invoiced post-event.

Early arrival requested beyond the standard 90-minute setup window is billed at 1.3x the standard rate ($100/hour per bartender).

9. CANCELLATION POLICY
• 10+ days before event: 75% refund (less deposit)
• 5–9 days before event: 50% refund (less deposit)
• Day of event or less than 5 days: No refund

All cancellations must be submitted in writing. If 513Sips must cancel due to emergency, injury, illness, or force majeure, all payments (including deposit) will be refunded in full.

10. INCLEMENT WEATHER

11. ALCOHOL, MIXERS & PRODUCTS
Alcohol Procurement: Unless Client has purchased the Alcohol Procurement Add-On, Client is responsible for purchasing and transporting all alcohol. Quantity guidance provided by 513Sips is a good-faith estimate only. 513Sips is not liable for shortages or overages.

If Alcohol Procurement Add-On is selected, 513Sips will shop for alcohol on Client's behalf using Client's budget. All alcohol is purchased at cost with receipts provided. Unused alcohol remains Client's property.

Mixers & Homemade Products: All mixers, syrups, and purees are prepared safely. Client must notify 513Sips of any allergies 14 days in advance. 513Sips is not liable for allergic reactions not disclosed in writing.

Licensing: Client must confirm the venue permits outside bartending services. 513Sips reserves the right to cancel with full refund if venue restrictions prevent lawful service.
