- $650 for 3hrs/50 guests = $4.33/guest (high for beer/wine, reasonable for craft cocktails)
- Signature cocktails at $150 each → ~$3/guest at 50 guests (HIGH if labor-intensive)

**Questions to Validate:**

2. What's competitor rate for mobile bars with mixology?
3. Do we include premium liquor in that $150 or is alcohol still client-provided?

- **Tier 1:** Beer/Wine: $650 (current) — Good
- **Tier 2:** Full Bar: $850 ($650 + $200) — Questionable value prop
- **Tier 3:** Signature Cocktails: Consider $100-125 (vs $150) unless high labor
