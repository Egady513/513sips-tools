Our Story

Crafting Memories, One Cocktail at a Time

513 Sips was founded with a simple mission: to bring the art of craft cocktails to every celebration. Based in Cincinnati, we combine professional expertise with a passion for hospitality to create memorable experiences.

Our team of certified bartenders brings creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail to every event. We handle everything from setup to cleanup, so you can focus on enjoying your celebration.
