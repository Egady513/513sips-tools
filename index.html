Our Story

From Vision to Reality

513Sips was born from Eddie's passion for bringing people together and creating memorable experiences. As a first-time business owner with a vision for elevated service, Eddie set out to build more than just a bartending company—he wanted to create moments that guests would remember long after the last drink was poured.

Operating under Elevated Ventures EG, LLC, 513Sips combines professional expertise with a personal touch. We understand that every event is unique, which is why we work closely with each client to craft a beverage experience that reflects their style and exceeds their expectations.

Based in Cincinnati and serving the Greater Cincinnati area, we're committed to delivering exceptional service with Christian integrity—treating every event as if it were our own.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Find answers to common questions about our mobile bartending services.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Greater Cincinnati area, including Northern Kentucky and Southeast Indiana. For events outside this area, please contact us for availability and travel fees.

Do you provide the alcohol?

Due to Ohio liquor laws, we cannot sell alcohol directly. However, we provide a detailed shopping list based on your selected menu and can assist with delivery coordination for an additional fee.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events. For smaller private parties, 4-6 weeks notice is typically sufficient. Last-minute bookings may be accommodated based on availability.

What's included in your service?

Our service includes professional bartenders, portable bar setup, all necessary bar tools and equipment, glassware (if requested), mixers and garnishes, ice, and complete cleanup. We handle everything so you can enjoy your event.

Can we create a custom cocktail menu?

Absolutely! We love creating custom cocktails that match your event theme or personal preferences. During our consultation, we'll discuss your vision and create signature drinks that will wow your guests.
