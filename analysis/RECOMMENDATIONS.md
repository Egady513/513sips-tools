## Executive Summary

After comprehensive analysis of 513sips.com and competitor cincysips.com, I've identified critical opportunities to transform your website from a basic Google Sites page into a professional, conversion-focused marketing asset.

**Current State:** C- (Google Sites template, poor SEO, no conversion optimization)  
**Target State:** A (Custom design, #1 SEO ranking, 20+ leads/month)

---

## Critical Issues Found

### 1. Platform Problems
- **Google Sites** - Limited customization, poor SEO, slow performance
- **Generic URL** - sites.google.com vs. custom domain
- **Template Look** - Unprofessional, hurts credibility

### 2. SEO Deficiencies
- Title tag: "Home" (no keywords)
- No meta description
- No H1 heading
- Missing schema markup
- No sitemap or robots.txt

### 3. Conversion Gaps
- No contact form (only email link)
- No phone number visible
- No pricing information
- No testimonials on homepage
- Weak CTA placement

### 4. Design Weaknesses
- Generic blue-gray colors (not your navy/gold brand)
- Poor typography hierarchy
- Low-quality images
- Not mobile-optimized
- No visual consistency

## Recommended Solution

### New Website Architecture

```
513sips.com (custom domain)
├── / (Homepage)
│   ├── Hero: "Elevate Your Event" + CTA
│   ├── Services Overview (3 cards)
│   ├── Social Proof (testimonials)
│   ├── Instagram Feed
│   └── Final CTA
├── /services
│   ├── Wedding Bar Service
│   ├── Corporate Events
│   └── Private Parties
├── /pricing
│   ├── Package Comparison
│   ├── Interactive Calculator
│   └── Add-ons
├── /gallery
│   ├── Photo Gallery (lightbox)
│   ├── Testimonials
│   └── Video
├── /about
│   ├── Founder Story (Eddie)
│   ├── Values (Christian focus)
│   └── Team/Bartenders
├── /faq
│   └── Common Questions
├── /contact
│   ├── Contact Form
│   ├── Phone/Email
│   └── Map
└── /blog (future)
    └── SEO Content
```

### Design System

**Colors:**
- Primary: `#1E3A8A` (navy)
- Secondary: `#CA8A04` (gold)
- Background: `#F8FAFC` (off-white)
- Text: `#1E40AF` (dark navy)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Great Vibes (elegant script)
- Body: Cormorant Infant (readable serif)

**Style:**
- Glassmorphism cards
- Generous whitespace
- Rounded corners (12px)
- Subtle shadows
- Gold accents on navy
