## 1. Platform Selection & Rationale

### Primary Platforms (Focus Here First)

| Platform | Priority | Why It Matters for 513 Sips |
|----------|----------|----------------------------|
| **Instagram** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Visual-first platform perfect for showcasing cocktails, event setups, and behind-the-scenes content. Primary discovery tool for wedding/event vendors. |
| **Facebook** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Essential for local business presence, Facebook Events integration, and reaching older demographics (parents planning weddings, corporate event planners). |
| **TikTok** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Growing platform for reaching younger couples (Gen Z/millennials). Short-form video content has high viral potential. |

### Platform-Specific Strategy

#### Instagram
- **Content Types:** Carousel posts (cocktail recipes), Reels (drink making, event highlights), Stories (daily behind-the-scenes)
- **Posting Frequency:** 4-5x/week feed, Daily Stories
- **Best Times:** 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm (EST)
- **Key Features:** Highlights for testimonials, Link in bio for booking

#### Facebook
- **Content Types:** Event photos, customer testimonials, local community engagement
- **Posting Frequency:** 2-3x/week
- **Best Times:** 1pm-3pm weekdays, 9am-11am weekends
- **Key Features:** Facebook Events, local group participation, reviews

#### TikTok
- **Content Types:** Quick cocktail tutorials, "Day in the life" content, trending audio with bartending twists
- **Posting Frequency:** 3-4x/week
- **Best Times:** 7pm-11pm (when users are relaxing/entertaining)
- **Key Features:** Duets with wedding content, trending sounds

## 5. Content Batching Workflow

### The 2-Hour Weekly Batch System

**Goal:** Create one week of content in 2 focused hours

#### Weekly Batching Schedule

| Time Block | Activity | Output |
|------------|----------|--------|
| **30 min** | Content Planning | Select 5-7 post ideas from content bank |
| **45 min** | Photo/Video Capture | Batch shoot cocktails, setup shots, B-roll |
| **30 min** | Graphic Creation | Design posts in Canva (use templates) |
| **15 min** | Caption Writing | Write all captions, add hashtags |

#### Monthly Deep Batch (4 hours, once per month)

1. **Plan Month Themes** (30 min)
   - Review upcoming events/holidays
   - Identify content opportunities
   - Map to monthly theme

2. **Batch Photoshoot** (2 hours)
   - Shoot 20-30 cocktail photos
   - Capture B-roll video clips
   - Photograph any new setups/props

3. **Create Templates** (1 hour)
   - Update Canva templates with seasonal colors
   - Create reusable quote graphics
   - Design story templates

4. **Build Content Bank** (30 min)
   - Organize photos in folders by category
   - Write 10 "evergreen" captions
   - Update hashtag sets

## 6. Engagement Automation Rules

### Response Time Goals

| Type | Response Time | Action |
|------|---------------|--------|
| Direct Messages | Within 2 hours | Personal response required |
| Comments | Within 4 hours | Like + reply to every comment |
| Story Mentions | Within 24 hours | Repost to your story |
| Reviews | Within 48 hours | Thank + respond personally |

### Engagement Automation (What You CAN Automate)

✅ **Automate These:**
- Welcome message in DMs (Instagram auto-reply)
- Away message during events
- Story reposts of tagged content
- Like comments automatically (some tools)

❌ **Never Automate These:**
- Comment replies (must be personal)
- Direct message conversations
- Review responses
- Engagement with potential customers

### Daily Engagement Routine (15 minutes/day)

**Morning (5 min):**
- Check DMs, respond to inquiries
- Reply to overnight comments
- Check story mentions, repost if appropriate

**Midday (5 min):**
- Engage with 10 target accounts (see below)
- Respond to new comments
- Post to Stories

**Evening (5 min):**
- Reply to end-of-day comments
- Check DMs one more time
- Plan next day's story content

### Strategic Engagement Targets

Engage daily with these account types (10-15 accounts):

1. **Local Wedding Vendors** (photographers, venues, planners)
2. **Recent Engaged Couples** (found via hashtags)
3. **Venues You Want to Partner With**
4. **Industry Peers** (other mobile bars, non-competing cities)
5. **Local Business Accounts** (build community presence)

### Engagement Scripts (Customize, Don't Copy)

**Responding to Wedding Content:**
> "Congratulations! Your venue is stunning 😍 Would love to be part of your celebration!"

**Responding to Venue Posts:**
> "This space is gorgeous! Would love to bring the bar to one of your events 🍸"

**Responding to General Comments:**
> Always use their name, reference something specific they said, ask a follow-up question.

## Final Tips for Success

1. **Consistency beats perfection** - Post regularly, even if it's not perfect
2. **Engage authentically** - Respond to every comment and DM personally
3. **Show your face** - People book people, not just services
4. **Track what works** - Double down on content that drives engagement and inquiries
5. **Be patient** - Social media growth takes 3-6 months to see real results
6. **Quality over quantity** - One great post beats three mediocre ones
7. **Use your events** - Every event is content goldmine - photograph everything
