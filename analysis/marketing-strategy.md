## 3. Marketing Channels

### Channel 1: Instagram (Primary)
**Strategy:** Visual storytelling + community building

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Event Showcases** (40%) - Photos from events
2. **Behind the Scenes** (20%) - Setup, prep, team
3. **Drink Recipes** (15%) - Signature cocktails
4. **Tips & Education** (15%) - Planning tips, trends
5. **User Generated** (10%) - Client photos/reposts

**Posting Schedule:**
- Feed: 4x per week (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun)
- Stories: Daily
- Reels: 2x per week

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 5-10 hashtags per post
- Mix of popular (#wedding) and local (#cincinnatiwedding)
- Branded: #513sips #uniquelyyours

**Instagram Bio:**
```
🍸 Premium Mobile Bar | Cincinnati
💒 Weddings | 🎉 Events | 🎂 Parties
✝️ Faith-Forward Business
📍 Serving Cincy, NKY, Dayton
👇 Book Your Date
[Link to contact form]
```

**Strategy:** Local SEO + reviews

**Posts:**

**Q&A:**

## 5. Pricing Psychology

### Current Pricing Structure
- Base: $650 (3 hours, 50 guests)
- Add-ons: Hourly, staffing, enhancements

### Recommended Positioning
**Good-Better-Best Options:**

**Essential Package** - $650
- 3 hours service
- 1 bartender (50 guests)
- Basic bar setup
- Standard mixers

**Signature Package** - $950  
- 4 hours service
- 2 bartenders (100 guests)
- Premium glassware
- 2 signature cocktails
- Custom theme decorations

**Luxury Package** - $1,450
- 5 hours service
- 3 bartenders (150 guests)
- Full bar upgrade
- 4 signature cocktails
- Premium decorations
- Arch backdrop

**Psychology Tactics:**
1. **Anchor High** - Show luxury first
2. **Middle Option** - Most popular (Signature)
3. **Value Stacking** - Show what's included
4. **Urgency** - "Limited dates available"
5. **Scarcity** - "Only 2 weekend slots left in October"
