## Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub Pages (Create Once, Link Often)
1. **Services Overview** - Link from every post
2. **Corporate Events Page** - Link from corporate-focused posts
3. **Private Parties Page** - Link from house party posts
4. **Pricing/FAQ** - Link when discussing logistics

### Linking Pattern
- First mention of "mobile bar" → Link to services overview
- Corporate context → Link to corporate events page
- Private party context → Link to private parties page
- Mention of pricing/packages → Link to pricing/FAQ
- Always include 2-3 internal links per post
- End each post with CTA linking to contact/booking

## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- Friendly but professional
- Helpful expert, not pushy salesperson
- Conversational but polished
- Cincinnati-aware (local references, neighborhoods, culture)

### Structure
- Hook in first 2 sentences
- H2 headers every 200-300 words
- Bullet points for scannability
- Image recommendations every 400 words
- Strong CTA at end

### CTA Templates
**Soft (Educational Posts):**
> "Planning an event in the Cincinnati area? We'd love to help you create something memorable. [Get in touch] to chat about your vision."

**Medium (Solution-Focused Posts):**
> "Ready to elevate your next event? Our mobile bar brings the experience to you—whether it's a corporate milestone or backyard celebration. [Check availability] for your date."

**Strong (Direct Conversions):**
> "Let's make your [event type] unforgettable. [Request a quote] and we'll get back to you within 24 hours."

- Video version → YouTube Shorts/Reels
